# Agentic Workflows

Agentic workflows split research into planning, execution, and synthesis loops.
The most reliable teams keep the intermediate artifacts inspectable so the model can
revisit its own trail of evidence and repair weak conclusions.

These workflows become much more useful when the outputs are filed into a durable
workspace instead of disappearing into a transient chat session.
