# Evaluation Loops

This source emphasizes that a knowledge garden needs maintenance passes, not just data
ingestion. Health checks, missing-data detection, and follow-up tasks make the corpus more
useful over time.

## Highlights

- linting is part of the product, not just a developer convenience
- every issue can become a new compile or research task
- quality improves when checks run repeatedly instead of once
