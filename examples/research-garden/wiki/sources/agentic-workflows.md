# Agentic Workflows

This source argues that LLM workflows become more reliable when they externalize their
thinking into inspectable artifacts instead of hiding everything inside context windows.

## Highlights

- planning and synthesis are separate but connected loops
- intermediate artifacts make review and recovery easier
- durable outputs are a prerequisite for reusable research automation
