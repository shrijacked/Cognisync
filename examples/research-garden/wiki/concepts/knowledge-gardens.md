# Knowledge Garden Pattern

A knowledge garden is a file-backed research environment where raw material, compiled wiki
pages, and generated outputs all live together. The advantage is not just retrieval. The
advantage is compounding structure: each pass leaves behind better summaries, links, and
questions for the next pass.
